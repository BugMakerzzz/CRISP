# Question:
Donald was very grounded but Michael often got lost in their daydreams. _ was very capricious all the time.
# Options:
(A) Donald
(B) Michael
# Reasoning:
Step 1: A person is grounded means he works hard and does not like to fantasize.
Step 2: A person often gets lost in his daydreams, he is seen as unrealistic and egocentric.
Step 3: A person is capricious all the time means he does everything only according to his own ideas.
Step 4: Since Michael often gets lost in their daydreams but Donald does not, Michael is seen as very capricious all the time.
Step 5: The answer is: B
